## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-260430-m7w-01 | I (Information Disclosure) | `prize.jpeg` served at `/prize.jpeg` from `static/` | accept | App is local-only (http://localhost:5001) per CLAUDE.md constraints. Flask is not exposed to the public internet. User confirmed this is a personal app for one recipient. Plan explicitly forbids adding any user-discoverable navigation/share targets pointing at `#preview-celebration` or the image, so the URL is effectively unguessable for any third party who might somehow reach the local server. |
| T-260430-m7w-02 | T (Tampering) | URL hash hatch in `main.js` | accept | Hatch is read-only — it triggers `showCelebrationScreen()` with mock client-side data and does not write to localStorage, the server, or any persistent state. `markBuildCompleted` is NOT called from the hatch path, so it cannot fake completion progress. |
| T-260430-m7w-03 | D (Denial of Service) | Vite static asset (96KB jpeg) | accept | One-time 96KB load on a personal localhost app — negligible. |
